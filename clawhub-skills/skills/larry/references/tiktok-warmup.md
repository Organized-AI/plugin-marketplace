# TikTok Account Warmup Protocol

## Why This Matters
New accounts that immediately post AI content get flagged as bots.
TikTok throttles their reach from day one. Warmup = real human signals.

## Duration
7–14 days minimum before first promotional post.

## Daily Activity (30–60 minutes)
1. **Scroll FYP naturally** — watch some fully, skip others. Don't watch everything — bots do that.
2. **Like sparingly** — ~1 in 10 videos in your niche only.
3. **Follow niche creators** — tells algorithm what the account is about.
4. **Watch niche content intentionally** — trains FYP to show your category.
5. **Leave a few genuine comments** — not spam, real reactions, 2–3/session.
6. **Optional: 1–2 casual videos** — nothing promotional, shows a real person exists.

## Ready Signal
When FYP is dominated by content in your niche → warmup complete.

## Post-Warmup Posting Cadence
- Week 1–2: 1 post/day
- Week 3+: 2–3 posts/day once algorithm learns your account
- Never post more than 3/day (spam signal)
